The fastest, easiest way for medical professionals to track their cases.

CaseCount offers an easy digital app that allows all medical professionals and students to effortlessly record and organize their medical cases, saving time and clutter. Moreover, CaseCount provides easy one-click reports, simplifying the sharing of case histories for insurance, liability, authorization, education and certification purposes. With data securely stored and easily accessible users can enhance their professional credibility, streamline administrative tasks, and experience newfound peace of mind.
